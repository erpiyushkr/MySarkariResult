CBSE Class 10th & 12th Result 2025

Important Information

The Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) has officially declared the Class 10th and 12th examination results for 2025. Over 30 lakh students across India can now check their subject-wise scores online. The board examinations were conducted between February and April 2025, with results being declared within 60 days of exam completion.

How This Page Helps Students

This page provides a comprehensive guide for checking CBSE results, with direct links to official portals. We help students understand their marksheets, calculate percentages, and navigate the verification process. Our resources save time and reduce confusion during result checking, while the FAQs address common concerns about revaluation, compartment exams, and document collection.

Important Dates

  • Class 12 Exam: 15 Feb - 2 Apr 2025
  • Class 10 Exam: 20 Feb - 30 Mar 2025
  • Result Date: 2 May 2025 (2 PM)
  • Revaluation Application: 3-15 May 2025
  • Compartment Exam: July 2025

Key Information

Frequently Asked Questions

1. When was the CBSE Class 10/12 Result 2025 declared?

The CBSE board declared Class 10 and 12 results on 2 May 2025 at 2 PM through their official website results.cbse.nic.in.

2. How can I check my CBSE result without roll number?

You can access results via DigiLocker using your Aadhaar number or contact your school for roll number details. UMANG app also provides alternative access.

3. What is the passing criteria for CBSE board exams?

Students need minimum 33% marks in each subject and aggregate to pass. For Class 12, practical and theory marks are combined for this calculation.

4. How to apply for CBSE marks verification?

Apply online through the CBSE website from 3-15 May 2025. Pay ₹500 per subject and submit the application through your school.

5. When will original marksheets be distributed?

Schools typically receive original marksheets within 3-4 weeks after online declaration. Contact your school administration for exact dates.

6. What if there's an error in my CBSE result?

Immediately notify your school principal who will contact CBSE for corrections. Keep screenshots of the online result for reference.

7. How to improve scores in compartment exams?

CBSE conducts compartment exams in July 2025 for students failing in 1-2 subjects. Register through your school by paying ₹300 per subject.

8. Is online marksheet valid for college admission?

Yes, colleges accept the online printout for provisional admission. Submit original marksheet when available for final documentation.

9. How to calculate CBSE percentage?

Add marks of best five subjects (excluding additional sixth subject) and divide by 5. For Class 12, include all subjects as per stream requirements.

10. What documents needed to collect original marksheet?

Carry school ID card and admit card. Some schools require signed acknowledgment slips. Parents may need to provide identity proof.
