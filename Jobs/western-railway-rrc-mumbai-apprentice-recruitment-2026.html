Eligibility Criteria ‚Äì Detailed

Educational Qualification

  • Passed 10th class (High School) examination with minimum 50% marks (aggregate) from a recognized board.
  • Possess ITI (NCVT/SCVT) certificate in the relevant trade for which apprentice is sought.
  • Candidate must have domicile of India.

Age Relaxation

  • SC/ST : 5 years
  • OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) : 3 years
  • PwD : 10 years (additional over category relaxation)
  • Ex-servicemen : As per government norms

Selection Procedure

Selection will be based on merit list prepared from the marks obtained in Class 10 (50% weightage) and ITI (50% weightage) or as prescribed in notification. No written exam. Shortlisted candidates will be called for document verification and medical examination.

  • Merit list = Average of 10th % and ITI % (as per trade).
  • In case of tie, older candidate is preferred.
  • Medical fitness as per railway norms.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs) ‚Äì Western Railway Apprentice 2026

1. What is the last date to apply for RRC WR Apprentice 2026?

Online application closes on 23 March 2026 up to 5:00 PM. Fee payment also closes on same day.

2. What is the application fee for female candidates?

Application fee for female candidates of all categories is NIL (‚Çπ0). SC/ST/PwD also have zero fee.

3. Can I apply without ITI certificate?

No, ITI certificate in relevant trade is mandatory along with 10th pass with 50% marks.

4. Is there any written exam for Trade Apprentice selection?

No, selection is purely based on merit (10th + ITI marks). No written test.

5. What is the age limit as on 23/03/2026?

Minimum 15 years and maximum 24 years. Relaxation as per rules: SC/ST 5 yrs, OBC 3 yrs.

6. Which division has the highest vacancies?

BCT (Mumbai) Division has highest vacancies: 1332 posts, followed by ADI Division with 1073 posts.

7. Can I apply for multiple trades?

You can apply for only one trade for which you possess ITI certificate. Option may be given in application.

8. What is the mode of stipend payment?

Stipend will be paid directly into bank account via ECS every month.

9. Is there any bond required after selection?

No bond is mentioned. Apprenticeship is for a fixed period (usually 1-2 years) as per trade.

10. What is the minimum percentage required in 10th?

Minimum 50% aggregate in Class 10 (High School). For SC/ST, may be relaxed as per notification.

11. Can candidates from other states apply for Western Railway Apprentice?

Yes, Indian nationals can apply. However, preference may be given to local candidates of respective division? Check notification.

Yes, candidates who have passed ITI before the application deadline are eligible.

13. What is the trade-wise vacancy list?

No, there is no written exam, hence no negative marking.

You can login again using your registration number and password to download the filled form before the last date.

16. What is the selection weightage of 10th and ITI marks?

Merit list is usually prepared by taking average of 10th percentage and ITI percentage (50:50). Confirm from notification.

Merit list likely to be released a few weeks after the application window closes. Keep checking RRC WR website.
