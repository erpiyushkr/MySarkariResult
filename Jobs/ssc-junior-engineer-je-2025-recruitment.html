Eligibility Criteria – Detailed

Educational Qualification

  • Degree Holders: BE/B.Tech in Civil/Electrical/Mechanical Engineering from a recognized university.
  • Diploma Holders: 3-year Diploma in Civil/Electrical/Mechanical Engineering from a recognized board with 2 years of relevant experience (where applicable).

Age Relaxation (as on closing date)

  • SC/ST : 5 years
  • OBC (Non-Creamy Layer) : 3 years
  • PwD : 10 years (additional over category relaxation)
  • Ex-servicemen : As per government norms

Selection Process

Selection for SSC Junior Engineer will be based on:

  • Tier I (Computer Based Test) – Objective type, 200 marks.
  • Tier II (Written Exam) – Conventional/Objective type, 300 marks.
  • Document Verification of shortlisted candidates.
  • Medical Examination as per SSC standards.

Final merit list will be prepared based on aggregate marks of Tier I and Tier II (normalized).

How to Fill SSC Junior Engineer 2025 Online Form – Step by Step Guide

Photo Instruction

The candidate will have to take a LIVE PHOTO for which a webcam will be required. There should be background light in the live photo and both the eyes of the candidate should be open and the photo should be absolutely straight.

  1. Step 1 – Registration: Visit the official SSC website (ssc.nic.in) or click the Apply Online link below. Click on “New Registration” and provide basic details (name, email, mobile). A registration number and password will be generated.
  2. Step 2 – Login & Fill Application: Log in using registration credentials. Fill in personal details, educational qualifications, category, and post preference (department-wise).
  3. Step 3 – Upload Documents: Scan and upload recent photograph (as per live photo guidelines), signature, educational certificates, etc. Ensure photo meets the specified dimensions and clarity.
  4. Step 4 – Preview & Verify: Before final submission, preview the entire form. Check all columns carefully. Corrections allowed only during the correction window (01-12 August 2025).
  5. Step 5 – Application Fee Payment: Pay ₹100 (if applicable) online via Debit Card/Credit Card/Net Banking or e-challan. SC/ST/Female candidates have zero fee but must complete the payment process.
  6. Step 6 – Final Submit & Print: After payment, click final submit. Save a copy of the application form and payment receipt. Take a printout for future reference.

Important Tips

  • Ensure live photo is taken in good lighting with both eyes open.
  • Keep scanned documents ready before starting form.
  • Fill educational details exactly as per certificates.
  • Apply well before the last date to avoid server rush.

Career Growth, Benefits & Job Value – SSC Junior Engineer

📈 Career Growth & Promotion Path

SSC Junior Engineers have a well-defined career progression. After joining, they can be promoted to the next grade (e.g., Senior Engineer, Assistant Executive Engineer) through departmental promotion committees (DPC) and limited departmental competitive exams. Promotion timelines typically range from 5-10 years depending on performance and vacancies. Higher positions include Executive Engineer, Superintending Engineer, and Chief Engineer in central engineering services.

🎁 Key Benefits & Allowances

This central government job offers job security, pension under NPS, medical facilities for self and family, house rent allowance (HRA) based on city of posting, dearness allowance (DA), transport allowance, leave travel concession (LTC), children's education allowance, and other perks as per 7th CPC. Employees also get periodic pay revisions and retirement benefits.

🧑‍💼 Who Should Apply

SSC JE is ideal for engineering graduates and diploma holders (with experience) who seek a prestigious and stable career in various central government departments like CPWD, MES, BRO, CWC, etc. Candidates with a strong technical background, problem-solving skills, and desire to work on infrastructure projects, public works, or defense engineering will find this role fulfilling.

📋 Job Role & Responsibilities

Junior Engineers are responsible for planning, designing, executing, and maintaining civil, electrical, or mechanical works in their respective departments. Tasks include preparing estimates, supervising construction, quality control, maintenance of equipment, project management, and ensuring adherence to technical standards and safety norms.

🌍 How This Job Helps Society

SSC JEs contribute to nation-building by developing and maintaining public infrastructure – roads, bridges, government buildings, water resources, defense installations, and more. Their work directly impacts the quality of life of citizens and strengthens the country's economic and security framework.

🚀 Long-Term Career Opportunities

Experience as a Junior Engineer opens doors to higher technical and administrative roles within the government. Engineers can later appear for promotions to Group A services (e.g., Central Engineering Service) or take up specialized roles in public sector undertakings (PSUs). Skills acquired are highly valued in both government and private sectors.
