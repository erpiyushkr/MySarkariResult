About Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL)

Bharat Heavy Electricals Limited (BHEL) is India's largest engineering and manufacturing company in the energy and infrastructure sector. Established in 1964, BHEL has been powering India's progress through world-class products and services.

Role of an Artisan at BHEL:
  • Perform skilled work in manufacturing processes
  • Operate and maintain specialized machinery
  • Ensure quality control in production
  • Follow safety protocols in industrial settings
  • Collaborate with engineering teams
