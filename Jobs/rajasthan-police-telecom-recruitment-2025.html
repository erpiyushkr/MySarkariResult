About Rajasthan Police Telecommunication Department

Department Overview

The Rajasthan Police Telecommunication Department is responsible for maintaining communication networks for the state police force. It plays a crucial role in:

  • Maintaining wireless communication systems
  • Operational support during emergencies
  • Technical support for police operations
  • Vehicle tracking and coordination
  • Maintenance of communication equipment

Constable Operator Role

  • Operation and maintenance of telecom equipment
  • Handling wireless communication systems
  • Technical support for police operations
  • Record keeping of communication logs
  • Assisting in emergency communication setups

Constable Driver Role

  • Driving and maintenance of police vehicles
  • Transportation of personnel and equipment
  • Assisting in emergency response situations
  • Maintaining vehicle logs and records
  • Ensuring vehicle readiness at all times

Selection Process

Stages of Selection

  1. Written Examination (OMR Based):
    • Objective type questions
    • Negative marking applicable
  2. Physical Efficiency Test (PET):
    • Running: 5km in 25 mins (Male), 5km in 35 mins (Female)
    • Height measurement
    • Chest measurement for male candidates (81-86 cm)
  3. Document Verification: Original certificate checking
  4. Medical Examination: Fitness test as per police standards

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the probation period for Rajasthan Police Telecom Constable?

The probation period is typically 2 years, during which performance is evaluated for confirmation in service.

2. Is there any interview for Telecom Constable selection?

No, the selection is based on written exam, PET, document verification and medical examination only.

3. Can candidates from other states apply?

Yes, but they must fulfill the educational criteria and age limit. Some posts may have domicile requirements.

4. What is the service bond for Telecom Constable?

Selected candidates need to serve minimum 5 years in Rajasthan Police or pay penalty as per rules.

5. How to prepare for Physical Efficiency Test?

Start with short runs and gradually increase distance. Practice daily to improve stamina and timing.

6. What is the posting location policy?

Selected candidates can be posted anywhere in Rajasthan as per department requirements.

7. Is there any relaxation in height for reserved categories?

No, height requirements are same for all categories as specified in notification.

8. How to prepare for Rajasthan GK section?

Focus on:

  1. Rajasthan history and culture
  2. Geography and climate
  3. Current affairs related to Rajasthan
  4. Important tourist places
  5. Folk arts and festivals

9. What is the work timing for Telecom Constable?

Generally 8 hours shifts, but may vary during emergencies or special operations.

10. How can I download my admit card?

Admit cards will be available on Rajasthan Police recruitment portal:

  1. Visit police.rajasthan.gov.in/recruitment
  2. Click on "Download Admit Card"
  3. Enter registration details
  4. Download and print admit card
