CSIR NBRI Recruitment 2025 - 30 JSA, Technical Assistant & Technician Posts

CSIR National Botanical Research Institute (NBRI) invites applications for Junior Secretariat Assistant (JSA), Technical Assistant and Technician positions.

  • Application Begin: 03/05/2025
  • Last Date: 02/06/2025 (upto 06:00 PM)
  • Exam Fee Last Date: 02/06/2025
  • CBT Exam Date: May/June 2025
  • Admit Card: Before Exam

  • SC / ST / PH: ₹0/-

How to Apply for CSIR NBRI Recruitment 2025

  1. Visit Official Website: Go to CSIR NBRI recruitment portal
  2. New Registration: Click on "Apply Online" and register with basic details
  3. Fill Application Form:
    • Personal Details
    • Educational Qualifications
    • Work Experience
    • Post Preference
  4. Upload Documents:
    • Recent Photograph (4.5cm × 3.5cm)
    • Signature (black ink on white paper)

  5. Payment of Fees: Pay through online mode (if applicable)

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the educational qualification for Technical Assistant posts?

The educational qualifications vary by discipline:

  • Option 1: Bachelor's Degree in Science from a recognized university in the relevant field
  • Option 2: Diploma in Engineering in the relevant discipline
  • For specific subject-wise requirements, candidates should refer to the official notification
2. Is there any relaxation in age limit for reserved categories?

Yes, age relaxation is provided as per CSIR NBRI rules:

  • SC/ST: 5 years relaxation
  • OBC: 3 years relaxation
  • PwD: Additional 10 years relaxation
  • Other categories as per government norms
3. What is the selection process for Technician posts?

The selection process typically includes:

  1. Computer Based Test (CBT) - Objective type questions
  2. Skill Test (for certain positions)
  3. Document Verification
  4. Medical Examination (if applicable)
4. Can final year students apply for these positions?

No, candidates must possess all required qualifications by the last date of application submission (02/06/2025). Final year students who will complete their qualifications after this date are not eligible.

5. What is the nature of job in CSIR NBRI?

CSIR NBRI offers:

  • Technical Assistant: Research support, lab work, data collection and analysis
  • Technician: Maintenance of equipment, field work, technical support
  • JSA: Administrative and clerical work in finance, stores, purchase departments
  • All positions are regular with probation period
6. Is there any interview for these positions?

For most technical and technician positions, selection is based on written test and skill test. However, certain positions may include interview as part of selection process. Candidates should refer to the official notification for post-specific details.

7. What documents are required during application?

Essential documents include:

  • Scanned photograph and signature
  • Educational qualification certificates
  • Experience certificates (if applicable)
  • Caste certificate (for reserved categories)
  • PwD certificate (if applicable)
  • Any other documents specified in the notification
8. What is the syllabus for the Computer Based Test?

The expected syllabus includes:

  • General Section: General Awareness, Reasoning, Quantitative Aptitude
  • Technical Section: Subject-specific knowledge based on the post applied for
  • Detailed syllabus will be provided in the official notification/exam notice
9. Are there any reservations for women candidates?

Yes, as per government norms:

  • 33% horizontal reservation for women in all categories
  • Application fee exemption for all female candidates
  • Special provisions for women employees as per CSIR rules
10. How can I get updates about exam date and admit card?

Candidates should:

  1. Regularly check the official CSIR NBRI website
  2. Check their registered email for updates
  3. Keep the application number safe for future reference
  4. Follow official social media channels if any
  5. Enable SMS/email notifications in application portal
