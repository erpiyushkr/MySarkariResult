About CSBC and Driver Role

What is CSBC?

The Central Selection Board of Constable (CSBC) is the authorized body responsible for recruitment of police constables in Bihar. Established to ensure transparent and merit-based selection, CSBC conducts examinations and selection processes for various constable positions in Bihar Police.

Constable Driver Role

The Constable Driver position in Bihar Police involves:

  • Operating and maintaining police vehicles (LMV/HMV)
  • Transporting police personnel and equipment
  • Ensuring vehicle readiness for emergency response
  • Maintaining vehicle logs and records
  • Assisting in law enforcement operations
  • Performing traffic duties when required

Career Insight:

Constable Driver is an excellent entry point into the police force with opportunities for promotion to Head Constable, Assistant Sub-Inspector (ASI), and higher ranks through departmental exams and merit-based promotions.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. Is LMV license sufficient for Bihar Police Driver recruitment?

No, candidates must possess both Light Motor Vehicle (LMV) and Heavy Motor Vehicle (HMV) driving licenses to be eligible for Bihar Police Constable Driver positions.

2. What is the salary for Bihar Police Constable Driver?

The pay scale is ₹21,700 - ₹69,100 (Level 3) with initial in-hand salary approximately ₹25,000 per month including various allowances.

3. Can candidates from other states apply?

Generally, only Bihar domicile candidates can apply. However, SC/ST candidates from other states may apply as per government rules. Check official notification for details.

4. What is the probation period for Constable Driver?

The probation period is 2 years during which performance will be evaluated for confirmation in service.

5. How to prepare for the driving test?

Focus on:

  • Vehicle handling in different conditions
  • Parallel parking and reverse driving
  • Knowledge of traffic rules and regulations
  • Basic vehicle maintenance knowledge
  • Practice with both light and heavy vehicles
