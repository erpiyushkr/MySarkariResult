About AIIMS and Nursing Officer Role

What is AIIMS?

The All India Institute of Medical Sciences (AIIMS) is a group of autonomous public medical colleges of higher education in India. Established by an Act of Parliament, AIIMS institutions are known for their excellence in medical education, research, and patient care. AIIMS conducts the Nursing Officer Recruitment Common Eligibility Test (NORCET) to select qualified nursing professionals for various AIIMS institutions across India.

Role of Nursing Officer

Nursing Officers at AIIMS play a critical role in healthcare delivery. Key responsibilities include:

  • Providing comprehensive nursing care to patients
  • Administering medications and treatments as prescribed
  • Monitoring patient conditions and vital signs
  • Assisting physicians during medical procedures
  • Maintaining accurate patient records and documentation
  • Educating patients and families on health management
  • Supervising junior nursing staff and trainees

Career Insight:

Working as a Nursing Officer at AIIMS offers a prestigious career in healthcare with opportunities for professional growth, specialized training, and participation in cutting-edge medical research. AIIMS provides excellent working conditions and opportunities for career advancement.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is the difference between NORCET Stage I and Stage II?

Stage I is a screening test that qualifies candidates for Stage II. Stage II is the main examination that determines the final merit list. Only candidates who clear Stage I are eligible to appear for Stage II.

2. Is there any interview for AIIMS NORCET selection?

No, there is no interview for NORCET selection. The selection is based solely on the Stage II examination marks. Candidates who qualify Stage II are called for document verification.

3. Can final year B.Sc Nursing students apply for NORCET?

No, only candidates who have completed their B.Sc Nursing or Diploma in GNM and are registered with the Nursing Council are eligible to apply. Final year students must complete their course and registration before the application deadline.

4. What is the validity of NORCET score?

The NORCET score is valid for recruitment in AIIMS institutions for one year from the date of declaration of results. Vacancies during this period are filled from the merit list.

5. Are there any reservations for female candidates?

Yes, AIIMS provides 30% horizontal reservation for women candidates across all categories. This means 30% of seats in each category (General, OBC, SC, ST) are reserved for women applicants.

6. What is the probation period for Nursing Officers at AIIMS?

The probation period for Nursing Officers at AIIMS is typically 2 years. During this period, candidates undergo orientation training and their performance is evaluated. Confirmation in service is subject to satisfactory completion of probation.

7. Can candidates from private nursing colleges apply?

Yes, candidates from private nursing colleges can apply provided their institution is recognized by the Indian Nursing Council or respective State Nursing Council, and they possess valid registration.

8. What is the career growth path for Nursing Officers at AIIMS?

Nursing Officers can advance to:

  • Senior Nursing Officer
  • Nursing Superintendent
  • Assistant Nursing Superintendent
  • Deputy Nursing Superintendent
  • Chief Nursing Officer
Opportunities for specialized roles in ICU, OT, and other departments are also available.

9. Is work experience mandatory for all candidates?

Work experience is mandatory only for candidates applying with a Diploma in GNM. B.Sc Nursing graduates do not require work experience. Diploma holders need 2 years of experience in a 50+ bedded hospital.

10. What is the training period for selected candidates?

Selected candidates undergo a mandatory training program of 6 months at AIIMS. The training includes:

  • Hospital orientation
  • Advanced nursing procedures
  • Specialty department rotations
  • Emergency response training
  • Professional ethics and communication
